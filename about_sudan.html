About sudan

It is an Arab country located in northeastern Africa. It is bordered by Egypt to the north, Libya to the northwest, Chad to the west, the Central African Republic to the southwest, South Sudan to the south, Ethiopia to the southeast, Eritrea to the east,
and the Red Sea to the northeast.

The documented history of Sudan begins about 50 centuries ago, or five thousand years

and the sources of this history are: Nubian inscriptions in some parts of Sudan.

the history of the region that constitutes present-day Sudan extends back to ancient times

when it witnessed the Kerma civilization (c. 2500 BC - 1500 BC)

then came under the control of the New Kingdom of Egypt for about five centuries

followed by the rise of the Kingdom of Kush (c. 785 BC - 350 AD),
which in turn controlled Egypt for nearly a century
